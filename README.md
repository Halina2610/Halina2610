## 👩‍💻 About Me

My journey in technology started more than 10 years ago when I built my first websites for educational and social organizations.

In 2020, I created my own e-commerce project and realized that software development was more than just a hobby. In 2022, I began studying web development professionally and gradually grew from a Frontend Developer into a Full-Stack Engineer.

Today I work across the entire technology stack — from user interfaces and APIs to infrastructure, deployment pipelines, monitoring, and production operations.

I enjoy solving complex problems, learning new technologies, and understanding systems as a whole rather than focusing on a single layer of the stack.
